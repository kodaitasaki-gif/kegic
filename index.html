Our Story

THE ART OF
Impossible

KEGIC was founded in 2019 by a team of working professional magicians who believed the industry deserved better tools. We obsess over every detail — from card stock weight to gimmick tolerances measured in fractions of a millimeter.

2019
KEGIC founded in Chicago. First product: our signature marked deck, handmade in batches of 100.
2020
Expanded to coin magic. Our Palmed Quarters sold out in 48 hours on launch day.
2022
Reached 10,000 customers. Launched the KEGIC Academy — free tutorials for our community.
2024
Opened our first physical studio. Products now available in 40+ countries.
Today
40,000+ magicians worldwide. Over 180 products. Still obsessing over every detail.
